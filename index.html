Royal Game of Ur

How to Play

The Royal Game of Ur is an ancient race game. Each player has 7 pieces that travel along a fixed path of 20 squares. On your turn you roll 4 binary dice (each die shows 0 or 1, so the roll is 0–4) and use the result to advance one piece.

If you land on a rosette (marked with a red circle), you get an extra turn. Landing on an opponent’s piece (unless that square is a rosette) captures it, sending it off-board.

Exact rolls are needed to finish the path. The first player to move all pieces off the board wins!
