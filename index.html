Royal Game of Ur

How to Play

The board is based on a 3×8 grid with gaps so that only 20 squares exist. Some squares are marked as rosettes (they get a fancy star design). On your turn, roll four binary dice (yielding 0–4) and move one of your 7 pieces forward.

Landing on a rosette grants an extra turn. You cannot capture an opponent on a rosette, and you must have an exact roll to exit the board.
