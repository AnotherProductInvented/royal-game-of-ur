The Royal Game of Ur

How to Play

The Royal Game of Ur is an ancient race game. Each player has 7 pieces that enter the board and follow a fixed T‐shaped path. On your turn you roll 4 binary dice (each die shows 0 or 1, so the roll is 0–4). Use the roll to move one piece forward along your path.

If you land on a rosette (marked on the middle row), you get an extra turn. If you land on an opponent’s piece (unless it’s on a rosette), you capture it (sending it off-board).

Exact rolls are needed to finish the path. The first player to move all pieces off the board wins!
