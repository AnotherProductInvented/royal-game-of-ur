Royal Game of Ur

How to Play

The Royal Game of Ur is an ancient race game. Each player has 7 pieces that move along a path of 20 squares (labeled 0–19). On your turn, roll four binary dice (0 or 1) and use the total (0–4) to move one piece forward. Landing on a rosette (red circle) grants an extra turn. Landing on an opponent’s piece (unless it’s on a rosette) captures it.

To win, move all 7 of your pieces off the board (past square #19). Exact rolls are required to leave the board. Good luck!
